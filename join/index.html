Join us

We build AI and control systems that run on real equipment, in real buildings, data centers, and industrial cooling plants. If you’re a student or researcher who wants to learn from real systems, contribute to live industry pilots, and build portfolio-level work that actually matters, we’d love to work with you.

What You’ll Get

  • Real-world data and hardware, not just simulations
  • Hands-on projects that can become strong portfolio pieces
  • Industry collaborations with companies in UK, EU, and Southeast Asia
  • Opportunities to co-author papers, reports, or open-source tools
  • Experience working on problems that have direct climate and energy impact
  • Mentorship in AI, control, optimization, and energy systems

You’ll build things that matter—and you’ll learn faster than in a typical academic group.

Research Topics

We’re actively looking for students and researchers to work on these areas:

HVAC & Industrial Cooling

Model predictive control and reinforcement learning for chilled-water plants, cooling towers, and building HVAC systems.

How to Apply

Email us with:

  1. A short introduction and your research interests
  2. Which topic you want to work on
  3. Your availability and preferred duration
  4. Your CV or links to relevant work/projects

What we look for: Curiosity, hands-on mindset, Python skills, and interest in control/ML/energy systems.
